About

Pelosi ETP follows publicly disclosed trading activity associated with Nancy Pelosi and other U.S. politicians, using those disclosures as signals for policy-linked equity exposure.

The product focuses on politicians whose portfolios have historically leaned toward technology and policy-sensitive sectors. Pelosi retires in January 2027, leaving more than a year of disclosed trading signals to follow, while the portfolio can also draw inspiration from other politicians.

Signal sourcePublicly available filings, disclosure updates, and theme-specific portfolio rules.
Rules-based trackingTransparent basket selection, NAV methodology, and periodic rebalancing framework.
Controlled accessMint, redeem, and secondary trading through one onchain ETP interface.

Where do we get these positions

Positions are constructed from publicly available disclosures, verified market data, issuer filings, and Alloco’s product methodology. Each theme is converted into a rules-based basket, then reviewed through liquidity, concentration, eligibility, and NAV calculation checks before it appears in the product interface.
